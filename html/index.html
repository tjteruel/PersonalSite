Out Distancing

Skill Distillery Final Project

Out Distancing was created with the intention of promoting social distancing activities and events outdoors. During this global pandemic and with strict social distancing guidelines, we encourage people to explore Colorado's natural beauties that are expansive, mountainous and serene. Undertake a new activity, join events or just traverse the diverse terrains. In the spirit of exploration, we hope our idea helps you to meet social distancing equanimously.

Quarantine Escape

Skill Distillery Midterm Project

We chose to create this specific application because the world is going through an unprecedented health crisis that has forced many of us to be self quarantined at home. This quarantine does not mean that socialization should end, there should be a virtual escape. Quarantine Escape will allow users to get out virtually and connect with other users while also doing their part to slow the spread of the Coronavirus.
